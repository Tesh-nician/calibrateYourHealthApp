Welcome to Calibrate Your Health

This is a platform for establishing baseline health parameters.

The aim of this platform is to provide a maximum benefit for relatively low effort.

To ensure optimal assement of your medical condition, health providers need to know what is normal for YOU. Otherwise they have no choice than to use statistics from a large general population.

All you need to do is record a few basic measurements once or twice a month, or even once a year.

Quit probably you already do this, but now you can do this in a more organized manner while getting all the benefits of long-term monitoring.
